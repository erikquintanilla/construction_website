Our Story

30 Years.
Oakland Built.

Founded in 1994, Atlas Construction has been a trusted partner for residential and commercial projects throughout Oakland, Berkeley, Piedmont, and Alameda. What began as a small family operation has grown into a respected construction company known for reliability, craftsmanship, and integrity.

We've overseen 500+ projects, from kitchen remodels to full commercial buildouts. Our success comes from a simple philosophy: honest pricing, skilled tradespeople, and work that lasts decades.

Every member of our team is licensed, bonded, and trained in the latest building codes and sustainable practices. We take pride in leaving every job site clean and every client satisfied.
