Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

Atlas Construction collects information you voluntarily provide through our contact form and other interactions. This includes your name, email, phone number, and project details.

We use the information you provide to respond to your estimate requests, deliver our services, and improve our website. We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties.

We implement industry-standard security practices to protect your information from unauthorized access. However, no online transmission is 100% secure.
